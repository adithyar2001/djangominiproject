ABOUT US

Welcome to EVAGO, where luxury meets comfort and exceptional service is our top priority. Nestled in the heart of Kochi, our hotel offers a unique blend of modern amenities and traditional hospitality to ensure an unforgettable stay for every guest.

OUR STORY

Established in 2024, EVAGO has been a cornerstone of hospitality in Kochi for a year. From our humble beginnings as a family-owned inn to our current status as a renowned luxury hotel, we have remained committed to providing unparalleled experiences to our guests.

MISSION AND VALUES

At EVAGO, our mission is simple: to exceed the expectations of every guest by delivering personalized service, exceptional accommodations, and memorable experiences. Our core values of integrity, excellence, and innovation guide everything we do, ensuring that each guest feels valued and appreciated throughout their stay.
